 Introduction

         The main WOVEN communication mode I articulated in this assignment for improvement in the following semester is electronic communication, which I enjoyed when completing a few interesting projects in my ENGL 1101 class. During the course of producing this artifact, to mix in specific details, I carefully reflected on my meaningful experience with this particular mode in the previous semester’s class. I found myself highly motivated to convey my thoughts through genres such as videos and online posts, but sometimes unsure about the best way to engage audiences because of the numerous possibilities. Interestingly, as an example of electronic communication, this diagnostic video also directly challenges me to practice my electronic communication skills necessary for composing my project. While coming up with the script, I not only kept track of its length but also considered which scenes to use for illustration purpose later in the editing phase. I decided to render my video by a combination of clips and animations with interactive still images. To accomplish these effects, I resorted to and sharpened my techniques using iMovie. After exploring a few possibilities, I made a few adjustments including timing, transition, and sound enhancement in the revision stage.

Recording Screen

To include multimodality in my production as well as to increase the diversity of the sources of the video content, I included a screenshot of me searching on the web as one of the clips.

Editing Video Using iMovie

iMovie is the main video editor I used for this project. With previous experience from ENGL1101 course using the same application for creating other videos, I further familiarized myself with its advanced features.
